Wuzee

Tufts Chinese Dance Troupe

Mission Statement

Wuzee (舞姿: a dancer’s aura/energy) is a Chinese fusion dance group, combining a variety of traditional Chinese styles and techniques with contemporary genres like modern and ballet. Founded in 2014, Wuzee has since grown from a subgroup of Tufts Chinese Student Association (CSA) to an independent performance group where we aim to not only explore our own identities in a new light but also spread appreciation of all forms of Chinese culture to our audiences. Since 2016, Wuzee has held 3 amazing showcases and has been seen on the stages of CSA’s New Year Gala, International Club’s Parade of Nations, Tufts Ballroom Team’s Winter Showcase, Tufts Singapore Student Association’s Culture Show, Boston Library, etc.
We are a rapidly growing group and welcome dancers of all levels, backgrounds and paths of life! Many of us started from scratch, but soon fell in love with the joy of communicating through dance. You may sign up for any dances that interest you which are all taught by senior members or professional coaches! If you are an experienced dancer, we welcome your solo performances and choreography ideas.
